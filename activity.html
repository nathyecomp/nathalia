AI Role-Playing Activity for High School Students

Objective: Explore the use of Artificial Intelligence in future professions.

Groups: 5-6 students.

Roles: Students role-play as future professionals using tools like ChatGPT-4.

Professions: Include doctors, engineers, lawyers, and creative professionals.

Tasks: Use or pretend to use AI for tasks like image generation, interpretation, spreadsheet analysis, text translation. Reflect on how AI tools will shape future professional roles. Identify and discuss concerns like bias, security, and privacy.

Presentation: 5-minute group presentations as future professionals. Address critical questions on AI ethics for their chosen profession. Some students act as clients with varied profiles.

Duration: 1 hour.

Note: Students must select one scenario to present or create a novel one.

Examples of Scenarios

1. Journalist

Scenario: Writing a report about the life of newcomers in Canada, with texts in different languages.

Client Profile: Editor of a news agency.

AI Usage: Gather background information, verify facts, and draft article outlines.

Critical Questions: Can AI bias influence news reporting? How does AI handle the confidentiality of sensitive information?
