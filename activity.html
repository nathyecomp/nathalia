AI Role-Playing Activity: Pretend that you are a professional in the future, using AI tools like ChatGPT-4 to support your work. How do you think jobs will change with such tools? What could be the good and bad sides?

Objective: Explore the use of Artificial Intelligence in future professions.

Groups: 5-6 students.

Roles: Students role-play as future professionals using tools like ChatGPT-4.

Professions: Include doctors, engineers, lawyers, and creative professionals.

Tasks: Use or pretend to use AI for tasks like image generation, interpretation, spreadsheet analysis, text translation. Reflect on how AI tools will shape future professional roles. Identify and discuss concerns like bias, security, and privacy.

Presentation: 5-minute group presentations as future professionals. Address critical questions on AI ethics for their chosen profession. Some students act as clients with varied profiles.

Duration: 1 hour.

Note: Students must select one scenario to present or create a novel one.

Examples of Scenarios

1. Journalist

Scenario: Writing a report about the life of newcomers in Canada, with texts in different languages.

Client Profile: Editor of a news agency.

AI Usage: Gather background information, verify facts, translate texts, and draft article outlines.

Critical Questions: Can AI bias influence news reporting? How does AI handle the confidentiality of sensitive information?

2. Doctor (Medical Diagnosis)

Scenario: Diagnosing a skin disease in a patient.

Client Profile: A patient.

AI Usage: Analyze patient symptoms, and medical history, and suggest potential diagnoses.

Critical Questions: How might AI bias affect diagnosis accuracy? What are the privacy concerns in using AI with patient data?

3. Lawyer (Legal Consulting)

Scenario: Advising on intellectual property rights for a startup.

Client Profile: Startup founders.

AI Usage: Research relevant laws, previous cases, and draft legal documents.

Critical Questions: How does AI ensure unbiased legal advice? What are the implications of AI in maintaining client confidentiality?

4. Marketing Specialist (Campaign Strategy)

Scenario: Developing a marketing campaign for a new product.

Client Profile: Product manager of a company.

AI Usage: Analyze market trends, generate creative slogans, and outline marketing strategies.

Critical Questions: Can AI-generated campaigns unintentionally propagate stereotypes? How is consumer data privacy maintained?

5. Film Director (Movie Script Writing)

Scenario: Creating a script for an upcoming movie.

Client Profile: Movie producers.

AI Usage: Generate script ideas, dialogue, and plot structures.

Critical Questions: How does AI influence cultural representation in scripts? What are the copyright issues with AI-generated content?

6. Fashion Designer (Trend Analysis and Design)

Scenario: Designing a new clothing line for an upcoming season.

Client Profile: Fashion brand CEO.

AI Usage: Predict fashion trends, suggest designs, and materials.

Critical Questions: Does AI reinforce existing fashion biases? How is the privacy of design ideas ensured with AI?

7. Financial Analyst (Investment Strategy)

Scenario: Developing an investment strategy for a client.

Client Profile: An individual investor.

AI Usage: Analyze financial data, predict market trends, and recommend investments.

Critical Questions: How does AI handle biased financial data? Are there security risks in AI-assisted financial analysis?

8. Kitchen Chef (Culinary Innovation)

Scenario: Creating new recipes and managing kitchen inventory.

AI Usage: Create new recipes, manage kitchen inventory, and analyze food trends.

Critical Questions: How does AI influence culinary creativity? What are the implications for traditional cooking methods?

9. Software Developer (AI Programming)

Scenario: Developing and refining AI algorithms for various applications.

AI Usage: Develop and refine AI algorithms, troubleshoot software issues.

Critical Questions: How do developers ensure AI algorithms are unbiased? What are the ethical considerations in AI software development?

10. Interior Designer (Space Optimization)

Scenario: Designing interiors for homes or businesses.

AI Usage: Create design models, analyze client preferences, and optimize space usage.

Critical Questions: How does AI affect design originality? What are the privacy concerns with client data?

11. Event Planner (Event Coordination)

Scenario: Organizing a large-scale international conference.

Client Profile: Corporate clients.

AI Usage: Organize events, manage guest lists, and personalize event experiences.

Critical Questions: Can AI fully understand cultural nuances in event planning? How is guest data privacy handled?

12. Sports Coach (Performance Analysis)

Scenario: Developing training regimes for a sports team.

Client Profile: Athletes and sports teams.

AI Usage: Analyze athlete performance, develop training regimes, predict injury risks.

Critical Questions: How does AI affect the coach-athlete relationship? What are the concerns regarding athlete data privacy?

13. HR Manager (Talent Acquisition)

Scenario: Recruiting for a tech startup.

Client Profile: Company executives and job applicants.

AI Usage: Screen resumes, predict candidate success, manage employee satisfaction.

Critical Questions: Can AI in HR processes lead to biased hiring? How is employee data privacy and security ensured?
